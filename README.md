CrowdPulse is an intelligent, real-time hospital bed management and emergency response platform designed to streamline healthcare coordination during both routine operations and critical emergencies.

The system connects patients, hospitals, and authorities through a unified digital infrastructure, enabling instant visibility into hospital resources, smart patient allocation, and rapid emergency response.

In high-pressure scenarios like accidents, disasters, or medical surges, MedPulse ensures that the right patient reaches the right hospital at the right time — minimizing delays and saving lives.

🚀 Key Features
🛏️ Real-Time Bed Tracking
Monitor availability across multiple hospitals with live status updates.
🤖 Smart Patient Allocation
Automatically assigns patients based on severity, distance, and hospital capability.
🚨 Emergency Reporting System
Enables instant incident reporting and rapid coordination with responders.
🚑 Integrated Emergency Response
Connects ambulances, hospitals, and authorities for seamless dispatch.
📊 Multi-Dashboard System
Dedicated interfaces for:
Public users
Hospital staff
Government authorities
📦 Resource & Inventory Management
Tracks critical medical resources like ICU beds, oxygen, and equipment.
📡 Live Monitoring & Alerts
Real-time updates, escalation alerts, and patient status tracking.
💡 Why MedPulse?

During emergencies, lack of coordination and visibility leads to critical delays.
MedPulse eliminates this gap by transforming fragmented information into structured, actionable intelligence.

🛠️ Tech Stack
Frontend: React, Next.js, Tailwind CSS
Backend: Node.js, Express
Real-Time: WebSockets (Socket.io)
Maps: Leaflet.js
AI Layer: Simulated NLP & decision engine
Database: Firebase / MongoDB / PostgreSQL

🎯 Vision
To build a scalable, intelligent healthcare coordination system that can be deployed across cities and countries, improving emergency response efficiency and ultimately saving lives.
